Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is modern technology that helps machines think and work like humans. AI tools are widely used in education, coding, business, and design.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI chatbot developed by OpenAI. It helps users answer questions, write code, and learn new topics.

Google Gemini

Gemini is an AI assistant developed by Google. It helps users with research, problem solving, and smart searching.

Claude AI

Claude AI is created by Anthropic. It helps users with writing, coding, and smart conversations.

Canva AI

Canva AI helps users create posters, presentations, and social media designs easily.

Future of AI

AI will become very important in healthcare, robotics, education, and smart technologies.

Advantages

AI saves time, improves productivity, automates tasks, and makes work easier.

Conclusion

AI tools are transforming the world with smart technology and helping humans in many fields.
